ABOUT ME


I’m studying Theoretical Physics at Trinity College Dublin. Alongside exploring the world of physics, I have a strong interest in coding, especially in areas related to Data Science and Statistics. I love building projects that uncover patterns and insights from data. When I’m not experimenting with code or physics problems, you’ll likely find me taking photos—capturing the world through my lens—or indulging in music creation. Whether it’s composing melodies or exploring new photographic perspectives, I’m always excited by opportunities to blend science, art, and technology.


I leverage powerful tools like NumPy and Pandas for efficient data manipulation and analysis, enabling seamless integration of complex mathematical and statistical computations into my projects. I specialize in building robust, scalable web applications using frameworks like Django and Flask, crafting solutions that are both functional and user-centric. Whether it's managing databases, creating APIs, or streamlining workflows, I thrive at turning ideas into impactful digital experiences.
